
This Service Level Agreement (SLA) outlines the support standards for applications published on the Atlassian Marketplace by Florenco.

## 1. Support Channels and Business Hours

All support requests are accepted through the official channel:
* **Email:** support@florenco.tech

**Support Business Hours:**
* Monday – Friday: 10:00 AM – 7:00 PM (EET/EEST)
* Saturday, Sunday: Closed

## 2. Incident Classification and Response Times

We aim to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible. The following maximum response time limits (during business days) apply:

| Priority | Description | Initial Response Time |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Critical** | The application is completely unavailable or a core feature is broken for all users. | up to 48 hours |
| **High** | Significant issues affecting major functions with no immediate workaround. | up to 5 business days |
| **Medium / Low** | General questions, UI bugs, configuration assistance, or feature requests. | up to 10 business days |

*Note: "Response Time" refers to the period between receiving the request and the initial response from a technical specialist. It does not guarantee a full resolution within this timeframe.*

## 3. Scope of Support

**What is included:**
* Assistance with installation and initial configuration.
* Bug fixes and troubleshooting application errors.
* Clarifications regarding app functionality.
* Updates to ensure compatibility with Atlassian product changes.

**What is not included:**
* General training for Jira or Confluence native features.
* Custom development or individual feature implementation for a single client.
* Support for third-party plugins or integrations.

## 4. Submission Guidelines

To ensure a swift resolution, please include the following in your initial request:
1. Application name and version.
2. Detailed description of the steps to reproduce the issue.
3. Relevant screenshots or screen recordings.
